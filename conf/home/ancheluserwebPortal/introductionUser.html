Anchel Web Portal

Launch any of the supported web browsers on your system and enter Anchel Link. in the address bar. The Anchel landing page is then presented.

  • Click on the User button to launch the landing page.
  • The login dialog will then be displayed. Enter the supplied credentials in the dialog.
  • After successful authentication, the home page will be displayed.
  • The overview of modules available include:
    • Copyright
    • Version of the application
    • Available storage space
    • User information
    • Preferences
    • Host your files
    • Activity logs
    • Upload and Share
    • Upload to a Workgroup
